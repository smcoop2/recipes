Homemade McMuffins:

ingredients:
1 english muffin
1 egg
1 slice american cheese(or your prefered cheese)
Optional: a small amount of butter to spead on english muffin

directions:
-Toast english muffin to desired level.
-In small bowl or large coffee mug break egg and whisk until yolk and egg whites are throughly mixed together. Adding a spoonful of water will make eggs more fluffy.
-Ticrowave egg for approximately 40 seconds or until the egg appears fully cooked and no luqid can be seen.
-Place egg on muffin and add cheese. Allow a few seconds for the cheese to melt and the eggs to cool off.
-Enjoy!
